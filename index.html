The Problem

Data without context is just noise.

Retail investors have more data than ever — yet remain at a severe informational disadvantage to institutional traders. The issue isn't access. It's interpretation.

📡

Information Overlap

Continuous streams of notifications, social media speculation, and clickbait news create cognitive overload — causing emotional, impulsive decisions.

📉

Context Deficit

When a CEO resigns or an SEC 10-K drops, investors receive the alert but lack the tools to understand its historical significance for that specific stock.

🏛️

Accessibility Gap

Institutional event-correlation tools providing this context cost $20,000–$30,000/year. Retail investors are left to perform hours of error-prone manual research.

How It Works

Three AI agents.
One conclusion.

A chained pipeline transforms a market event into a personalized, statistically-grounded recommendation in seconds.

$ python main.py --mode real
PIPELINE OUTPUT
Agent 1 → TSLA / CEO_CHANGE / HIGH
Agent 2 → 3 precedents found
Agent 2 → Avg 30d: −0.8% (MEDIUM conf.)
Agent 3 → Rebalance: −2% TSLA suggested
01

Build Your Watchlist

Add any of 25+ tracked tickers to your personalized watchlist. Sentiment indicators appear instantly, and the dashboard adapts to show only what matters to your capital.

02

Agent 1 Scans & Classifies

The Event Scanner filters 112+ event types and 35+ news sources against your watchlist — delivering HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW severity alerts with a notification badge and optional audio ping.

03

Agent 2 Maps History

Click any alert. The Historical Analyst instantly correlates the event type against 90 days of price data, calculates average 1-day, 7-day, and 30-day post-event returns, and generates a narrative with confidence level.

04

Agent 3 Personalizes the Advice

Your Risk Score — calculated from salary, savings, and age — filters the historical insight into a personal portfolio allocation recommendation. The same event means different things for a 28-year-old engineer vs. a 55-year-old executive.

05

Decide. Execute. Export.

Export a clean institutional-style PDF report of any analysis panel. Execute trades on your preferred brokerage. Brokerage API integration — Robinhood, eToro — is on the 2026 roadmap.
